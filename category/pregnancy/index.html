Pregnancy & Pediatric Tracking Hub

A safe, evidence-based space for expecting and new mothers. During pregnancy and postpartum, monitoring weight should be treated with high sensitivity and focus on health rather than restrictive dieting. Our tools help parents track normal newborn weight loss trends in the first days of life, check pediatric growth patterns, and plan postpartum weight management safely (automatically compensating for breastfeeding calorie requirements).

Maternal & Infant Weight Tools

  • Newborn Weight Loss Calculator - Calculate baby's initial weight loss percentage with clinical thresholds (5%, 7%, 10%).
  • Infant Weight Loss Tracker - Monitor pediatric growth trajectories and recovery timelines.
  • Baby Weight Loss Calculator - Compare baby's weight to WHO percentiles.
  • Postpartum & Breastfeeding Calculator - Set safe, supply-friendly caloric targets while nursing.

Dietitian-Reviewed Guides
