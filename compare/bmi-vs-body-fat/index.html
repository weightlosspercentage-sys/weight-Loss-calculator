BMI vs Body Fat Percentage: Which Matters More?

BMI is fast and free; body fat percentage is more accurate. Use both together for the most honest picture of your body composition.

Feature Comparison Table

Feature BMI Body Fat Percentage
Inputs Height, weight Circumferences or scan
Distinguishes muscle vs fat? No Yes
Accuracy Population-level screen Individual composition
Time to measure 10 seconds 2–3 minutes
Best for Quick check-in Tracking real composition change

Comparison Verdict

BMI is a useful five-second screen. For meaningful progress tracking — especially while training — body fat percentage wins.
