Starbucks Beverage Calorie & Sugar Counter

Starbucks offers a massive selection of coffee, teas, and specialty drinks. While black coffee contains virtually zero calories, specialty beverages are often loaded with milk, whipped cream, and sugary syrups.

For example, a Grande White Chocolate Mocha contains 430 calories and 53 grams of sugar. You can customize drinks by requesting non-fat milk, almond milk, or sugar-free syrups to enjoy your favorite beverages with a fraction of the calories.

This calculator lets you configure your favorite Starbucks drink, choose sizes and milk types, and see the exact calories and sugar content instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I lower calories in a Starbucks drink?

Choose non-fat milk or unsweetened almond milk, skip the whipped cream, and ask for fewer pumps of syrup or sugar-free syrup.

Q: How many calories are in a Starbucks sugar-free syrup pump?

A single pump of Starbucks sugar-free syrup (like vanilla) contains 0 calories, whereas regular syrup contains about 20 calories per pump.
