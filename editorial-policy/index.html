Editorial Policy

Our Commitment to Evidence-Based Health Information.

Medical Accuracy and Review Process

All content published on Weight Loss Percentage, including our calculators, guides, and articles, is strictly vetted for medical and scientific accuracy.

Our editorial team ensures that:

  • Every claim regarding weight loss, nutrition, or hormonal health is backed by peer-reviewed studies or clinical guidelines.
  • Our calculators are developed based on established scientific formulas (e.g., Mifflin-St Jeor, Harris-Benedict) and updated as clinical consensus evolves.
  • Medical disclaimers are visibly present, ensuring users understand our tools are for educational purposes, not clinical diagnosis.

Author Qualifications

We work with credentialed experts, including Certified Personal Trainers (CPTs) like Sarah Jenkins, to ensure our fitness and weight loss content reflects practical, safe, and effective methodologies.
