Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What does a BMI of 69.0 mean for someone 4'10" tall?

A BMI of 69.0 for a 4'10" individual classifies you as Obese Class III. Class III (severe) obesity poses the highest risk for premature mortality, severe cardiovascular disease, and complications from metabolic syndrome. Bariatric evaluation may be appropriate.

Q: What is the ideal weight for someone 4'10" tall?

For a height of 4'10", the healthy weight range is 88 to 119 lbs (BMI 18.5 to 24.9). Use our BMI calculator to check any weight.

Q: How is BMI calculated for 4'10" 330 lbs?

BMI = (weight in lbs ÷ height in inches²) × 703. For 4'10" (58 inches) and 330 lbs: (330 ÷ 58²) × 703 = 69.0.

Q: How can I lower my BMI from 69.0 to a healthy range?
