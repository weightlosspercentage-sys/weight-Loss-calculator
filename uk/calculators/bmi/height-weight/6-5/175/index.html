Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What does a BMI of 20.7 mean for someone 6'5" tall?

A BMI of 20.7 for a 6'5" individual classifies you as Normal Weight. A normal BMI range is associated with the lowest risk of weight-related health problems. Maintain this range through balanced nutrition and regular physical activity.

Q: What is the ideal weight for someone 6'5" tall?

For a height of 6'5", the healthy weight range is 156 to 210 lbs (BMI 18.5 to 24.9). Use our BMI calculator to check any weight.

Q: How is BMI calculated for 6'5" 175 lbs?

BMI = (weight in lbs ÷ height in inches²) × 703. For 6'5" (77 inches) and 175 lbs: (175 ÷ 77²) × 703 = 20.7.
